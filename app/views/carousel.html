We love book readings. We find them fascinating. We were becoming increasingly frustrated by combing the corners of the internet to find readings on obscure websites in terrible formats. We thought they were much too important. We designed a place for all of them to live together. We would love if you would help us achieve this dream!

What the heck are book readings?

Book readings, otherwise known as “book talks”, “book events,” or simply "readings", all follow a similar format. Authors embark on book tours where they stop at book stores and libraries around the country, read a selection from a recently published book, do a question and answer session with the audience, then (usually) sign copies of their book. The Q&A session is where the most interesting stuff lives (in our opinion) and is one of the reasons we fell in love with readings.

If you haven’t listened to a reading or been to one, you aren’t alone. Authors tend to follow a well-trod path of cities. This is another reason why we feel so passionate about making them available to all and bringing them the recognition we think they deserve.
